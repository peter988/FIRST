About Us

First Supplies Import & Export is the American subsidiary of a successful Egyptian parent company, with a focus on importing and exporting automotive parts. With Ms. Hana’s innovative leadership and Mr. Farag’s operational expertise, the company is poised to capitalize on the burgeoning market for automotive parts in the United States.

Business Operations

The company specializes in the import of truck parts from diverse international markets and the export of junk cars to strategic global locations. The U.S. entity will leverage the established networks and expertise of its Egyptian counterpart to gain a competitive edge in the American market.

Leadership Team

Margrett Hana, a business owner with extensive experience in web development and a strong foundation in business management, will lead as the CEO. She has successfully expanded the Egyptian business internationally, showcasing her expertise in banking, accounting, and global trade dynamics. Farag Farag brings over 20 years of experience in the industry and will be responsible for the daily operational management of the U.S. subsidiary. Their combined expertise forms a robust leadership team capable of driving the company to new heights.
